📌 **Overview**
This project visualizes key business and academic metrics for a student learning platform, helping stakeholders track revenue trends, course performance, enrollment patterns, and teacher effectiveness — all in one place.

🎯 **Key Metrics (KPIs)**
Total Revenue: ₹911.3K
Total Enrollments: 10K
Unique Students: 3K
Total Courses: 61
Avg Teacher Rating: 3.13 / 5
Avg Course Rating: 3.10 / 5

🖥️ **Dashboard Pages**
Overview – High-level KPIs, revenue trends, and category breakdowns
Student – Student demographics and enrollment details
Performance – Course and teacher performance analysis
Attendance – Student attendance tracking

📈 **Key Visuals**
Revenue by Gender & Age Group – Revenue split across gender and age brackets
Revenue by Course Category & Level – Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced breakdown per category
Monthly Revenue & Enrollment Trends – Combined line and bar chart showing trends across the year
Total Enrollments by Payment Method – Donut chart comparing PayPal, Credit Card, and Bank Transfer
Monthly Revenue & Enrollment Trends – Combined line and bar chart showing trends across the year
Total Enrollments by Payment Method – Donut chart comparing PayPal, Credit Card, and Bank Transfer
Revenue by Months & Course Category – Stream graph of revenue across categories over time
Top 5 Teachers by Avg Rating – Ranked bar chart of top-performing teachers

🛠️ **Tools & Technologies**
Power BI Desktop – Data modeling, DAX calculations, and visualization
DAX – Custom measures and calculated columns
Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation

🔍 **Features**
Interactive slicers for Course Category, Course Type, Gender, and Payment Method
Drill-through and cross-filtering across visuals
Dynamic KPI cards
Responsive layout for desktop and mobile view
